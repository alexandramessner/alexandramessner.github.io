Introduction

Crafted Coast

Crafted Coast is a vibrant beachy magazine that celebrates the art of craft beverages such as beer, kombucha, and cider. Catered towards young adults, it shines a spotlight on smaller breweries and makers, giving readers an insider's look into the world of craft brewing. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, Crafted Coast invites readers to discover the best locally made beverages while immersing themselves in the laid-back coastal lifestyle.
